Long Form Content Writing

Long form content like blogs and articles have a few prerequisites to be fulfilled. A kickass headline to draw the audience to the article, and words good enough to make them stay. I’ve worked on a variety of long form content, ranging from insurance companies to broadband services to financial divisions.


Copywriting

Copywriting, unlike content writing, demands to be crisp. In the world we live in today, the average attention span of our audiences have majorly reduced. Bringing 1000 words to 12 is truly an art. I have worked as a copywriter for one of India’s leading pub chains, primarily writing for their social media accounts.


Blog & article Editing

Editing a document or article is a lot more than just finding grammatical inaccuracies. Editing improves readability and the ability of the words to connect with the reader. Content can maximise its potential only when it strikes the right chords. I’ve worked as an editor (English section) for the award winning magazine Spectrum.
